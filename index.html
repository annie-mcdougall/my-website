A little about me

As a product designer (who is learning to code), Annie thrives off of figuring out solutions to common problems. Annie is driven by a desire to help people, and she is always eager to uncover what motivates users in order to create experiences which continue to engage over time, resolve user pain points, and delight and inspire along the way.

Over the past six years, Annie has worked in two disciplines across three continents, finding her professional footing in design and copywriting in the fast-paced world of news media. Annie worked as a Subeditor for the Australian Associated Press in New Zealand and Vietnam, before moving to Canada and transitioning to future-forward UX and UI Design, and now development.
